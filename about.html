About Kawalthe Stay

THE EXPERIENCE

What is Agro Tourism?

Agro Tourism is more than just a vacation; it is a return to your roots. It is about waking up to the chirping of birds, breathing air that hasn't been touched by city pollution, and eating food that comes straight from the farm to your plate.

At Kawalthe Stay, we bridge the gap between urban hustle and rural peace. We invite you to experience the authentic lifestyle of an Indian farmer, participating in farming activities or simply relaxing under the shade of ancient trees.

OUR HOME

Welcome to Kawalthe

Nestled in the lush greenery of the region, Kawalthe Stay is designed to be your second home. Our property reflects the traditional architecture of Maharashtra while providing modern amenities for a comfortable stay.

We are located near historical forts and temples, making us the perfect base for your explorations. Whether you want to trek the nearby hills or sit by the campfire sharing stories, Kawalthe Stay offers the perfect atmosphere.
