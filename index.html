The Kawalthe Experience

Reconnect with Roots

Escape the concrete jungle and embrace the tranquility of rural Maharashtra. At Kawalthe Stay, we bridge the gap between urban hustle and nature's rhythm. Experience authentic living in eco-friendly mud cottages, savor organic farm-to-table dining, and witness the timeless bond between man and soil.
